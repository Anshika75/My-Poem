My Poem


Waiting for you⏳
From day ⛅ to night🌃,
Seeking in darkness🌑
Your love❤ light🕯

Wanted to walk🚶‍♀️
Holding your fingers👇,
Wrapped arround my
Lonely hands👐

Wanted to close😣
These teary eyes🥺,
With only one wish🌠
For which I thrive
To see me lost with you👨‍👧
In our own paradise🌌

Wanted to address📢 you
With all those🗣 names🏷
That were silent🤫 by my cries😭,
Wanted to use all those rights©️
For which, being your child👶 I never fight🏳

Wanted to have
All those fun🥳,
That since a child👶
Are left undone😞

Wanted to play🤾‍♀️
In your arms💪,
Forgetting all of my harms⚠️⚠️

Wanted to scream🗣
For those ice-creams🍨🍦,
That once we had🍴
Down the stream🏞

Many say I have a killer🔪 smile😊
No one sees🧐 tears hidden behind😢,
Everyone👥 says I have lovely eyes👀
No one knows the reason of swollen red eyes🥺

I still remember😨 that scary night🌃
Never thought would be so unright⚔
And would be our last meet🤝,
It haunts me like a nightmare😱
Never knew that good bye....👋
Was meant forever♾
And I was seeing🧐 you for the last time⌛

This abode is no longer a home🏡,
Antiquated🏚 with your memories alone📜

My intuition🧐 says🗣
You🧔 are still somewhere near🔍
Then why this fear😨?
I still yearn😔
To be called by you as"dear"🥰
Your this act😟 always makes me tear🥀
